1

REAL-TIME UPDATE

what’s everyone working on

When you’re planning things with your team, the days get busy with scheduling, prioritizing and deadline-hunting. You’re rushed off your feet to keep track of all the project details, yet still you need to have the full overview.

Swipes is your sidekick on the task - presenting you a dashboard with a real-time update of what is everyone working on. A single look at it gives you the full clarity on the current priorities and it automatically updates as progress is made.

2

Proactive Planning

of the available time

When you’re working with your team, time often passes by in briefings and meetings. But you still have tons of tasks planned for the day and very little idea when, what to do and even - how much time it will take you.

With Swipes, you don’t save the day in the last moment, catching up on the overdue tasks. Instead you tap into a proactive system that understands your schedule for the day, the available time at hand and sends you one priority at a time.

3

FLEXIBLE SYSTEM

helping you cope with the unexpected

Even with the best overview in place, life gets in the way of planning. It is not ‘if’ but ‘when’ and your static project plan can’t possibly understand why the deadline got pushed with 2 weeks when the designer caught a cold.

Swipes understands that things get in the way. It plans for them, it knows the dependencies between your team mates and it informs you on-time how each change in the priorities will affect the whole outcome.

Swipes - the first teamwork tool that solves the complexity of achieving things together.
